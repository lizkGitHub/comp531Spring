Directions

  1. The score will count one point for each obstacle you manage to go through.
  2. Click the mouse to make owl flap upward. And it will keep going upward if you maintain mouse down.
  3. The difficulty of this game changes dynamicly over time. The speed of both owl and obstacle becomes faster every 20 seconds.
  4. The more obstacles you can pass with few clicks, the better record you will have.
  5. The best record is primarily based on the score (the number of obstacle). If the scores are same, the record with the less clicks is better.
